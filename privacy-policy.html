Privacy Policy

The Google Block Breaker website stands dedicated to privacy protection and secure management of your provided data as you use our platform. The Privacy Policy applies to all visitors using the website located at https://blockbreakergoogle.github.io/. Accepting our website's terms permits Google Block Breaker to gather and utilize information according to this policy.



Information We Collect



Our website obtains information that features two distinct types:

Non-Personally Identifying Information:
Our website logs standard information that most platforms collect, which includes browser type, language preference, visiting site information, and session timestamps. Collecting this information lets us study visitor behaviors to enhance their experience on our website.

Potentially Personally-Identifying Information:
Files containing Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, along with user login information, are collected from registered individuals and comment contributors on our website. The collected information receives proper care and handling, while disclosure happens only in particular circumstances that follow this explanation.

Security of Your Information



Your personal information safety is our highest priority. The security provided by internet and electronic storage methods cannot be guaranteed at 100 percent. We implement commercially acceptable protection techniques, yet the complete assurance of data security is not possible.



Cookies



The website relies on cookies to improve browsing experiences for users. Your device contains small files known as cookies, which track your website preferences and help us enhance our service offering. Turning off cookies in your browser might decrease the functionality of some features present on our website.



Third-Party Links and Ads



You will find external site links and third-party advertisements presented on our website. The third-party companies apply cookies to monitor your activities with the goal of delivering targeted advertisements. Before entering external sites, review their privacy policies because we cannot manage their content or privacy practices.



Google AdWords Remarketing



By using Google AdWords Remarketing, we reach potential audiences among people who have previously visited our website. Third-party websites and Google users can view advertisements for our services because of this feature. Our advertising content shows relevant information based on your history of visiting our website pages. To disable interest-based advertising, you should use browser plugins alongside modifying your Google Ad Preferences settings.



Disclosure of Information



Our organization refrains from selling or renting any personal identification data of our users. Our company shares this information under the following circumstances:

  • In response to legal requests, such as subpoenas or court orders.
  • We expose personal information for the sake of safeguarding our website, its users, and the public, along with their rights and possessions.


Aggregated Statistics



We process and share data that pertains to visitor activities on our website in the form of aggregated information that does not contain personal identifiers. The data reveals behavioral patterns that enable our organization to enhance service delivery without compromising user confidentiality.
